Which one of the following states in India receive a major part of its annual rainfall in winter.
A
Orissa
B
Madhya Pradesh
C
Telangana
D
Tamil Nadu
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -The regions that receive rainfall in winter in India are Tamil Nadu and Kerala. This is due to a phenomenon called North east monsoon.

Detailed explanation-2: -Among the highest rainfall states in India, feature Arunachal Pradesh, Sikkim, West Bengal, Odisha, Uttarakhand, Karnataka and Kerala.

Detailed explanation-3: -Due to the geographical location, the east coast receives rainfall as the north east trade winds blow from sea to land in this location. Hence the coast of Tamil Nadu receives winter rainfall.
