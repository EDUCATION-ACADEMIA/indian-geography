The ____ is the average weather condition measured over many years.

(A) Weather

(B) Temperature

(C) ** Climate

(D) Season

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(i) The average weather taken over a long time is called climate. Weather refers to short-term changes in the atmosphere, while climate refers to the weather in a certain place over a long period of time.

Concept note-2: -Climate is the average weather condition, which has been measured over many years.

Concept note-3: -Climate is the average condition of the weather in a place over a long period like 25 years and so. While the weather can change in just a few hours, climate takes hundreds, thousands, even millions of years to change. It characterizes a regionâ€™s general weather patterns that happen over the course of many years.

Concept note-4: -Climate describes the average weather conditions of a particular place over a 30 year period .

Concept note-5: -Climate is the average of weather conditions and variations over a large area for a long period of time whereas weather refers to the state of atmosphere over an area at a given point of time.
